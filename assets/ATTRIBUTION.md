# Image attribution

Craft, place, and portrait photographs under `assets/crafts/` and `assets/guides/` are from **Wikimedia Commons**. They depict Kenyan crafts (Maasai beadwork, Kisii soapstone, Kazuri beads, kiondo baskets, Lamu carving, Turkana jewelry, Akamba woodwork, etc.), Kenyan destinations, and Kenyan / East African people.

Original files remain under their respective Commons licenses (typically CC BY / CC BY-SA / public domain). Filenames are shortened for the app; search the original titles on Wikimedia Commons for license text and author credit.
